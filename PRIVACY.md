Privacy Policy

ChatGPT History Cleaner does not transmit any personal data.

All operations performed by the extension occur locally within the user's browser. 
The extension does not send any information to external servers.

The extension only modifies the display of ChatGPT conversation history to improve usability.

Performance mode may store static snapshots of hidden messages in the extension's local IndexedDB so they can be expanded later. These snapshots never leave your browser. You can clear them from the extension Settings page, and expired snapshots can be cleaned by the configured retention period.
