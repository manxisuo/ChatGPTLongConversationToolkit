Privacy Policy

ChatGPT Conversation Toolkit does not transmit any personal data.

All operations performed by the extension occur locally within the user's browser.
The extension does not send any information to external servers.

The extension modifies the display of ChatGPT conversations and provides local navigation tools. It does not upload conversation content, collect analytics, or store full conversation snapshots.

When the user creates a bookmark, the extension stores the conversation identifier, message identifier, message role, a short message preview, and bookmark timestamp in `chrome.storage.local`. This bookmark data remains in the user's browser and can be removed by the user.
